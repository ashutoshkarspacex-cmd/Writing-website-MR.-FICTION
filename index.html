Our Story

Born from a
teenager's dream.

This page is the dream of a teenager from a small town — an idea born from a deep love for words and stories. It's an attempt to bring together aspiring writers from around the world, creating a platform that celebrates free will, creativity, and imagination.

At the heart of this space is independent thinking. That's what we strive to nurture, one story at a time.

We live in the age of AI — a time when the internet is overflowing with recycled ideas and altered versions of once-original works. While technology has its place, it has also made it harder to find the raw, unfiltered voice of a human mind at work.

Here, we take a different path. Every word you read on this platform is born from genuine human thought — crafted with intention, shaped by real emotions, and unpolished in all the ways that make writing truly alive.

We don't just want content. We want ideas. We want the spark that comes from a living, breathing person putting pen to paper and letting their mind roam free.

A Divine Request — Indian Independence Day Special

@HINDUTV · Independence Day Edition · Mr. Fiction Zone

15 August, 2025 Dear Man, Hello my dear followers! Hope you all are doing awesome. Thanks for all the birthday wishes – I hear my Indian subscribers are also celebrating Independence Day today. Congratulations! Seventy-nine years, I hear – truly a long time since Jesus’s followers “gave” you freedom. The torture and torment you all had to endure—200 years of colonization—is no joke! You must now be roaming free, singing songs of independence enthusiastically. Let’s talk frankly today—it’s been days since I last contacted you. Your women must be self-sufficient, able to tread without fear – venturing freely at night just like men, right? Your government must be highly efficient—obviously, it must be, because you worship your ministers nowadays, ha-ha! Infrastructure must be highly advanced – not even a doubt, since your ancestors documented every scientific, medical, and mathematical theorem—ancient knowledge, right? I hope you are utilizing it well! People must be well-behaved, with great civic and ethical sense, right? Unemployment must not exist, right? Of course, why should it – people are highly literate, right? And also, our corona content must have taught you well to protect and preserve nature—you call it “mother,” right? Of course, who would not care for their mother? I also hope there is proper law and order in the nation—surely, rich people would not be getting away with heinous crimes through bribery. It was truly pleasant to see all the rape victims being given justice – with perpetrators being brutally punished. It was also nice to see how even eminent celebrities get jailed for trampling homeless people on the footpath. And the way that judge in Pune handled the Porsche crash case—I was so delighted! Only in India could it have occurred. You know, in other countries for such cases, when rich juveniles are involved, they are merely asked to write a 1000-word essay on road safety! Such a meagre punishment only because they were rich! But only in the great land—Bharat—will you get true justice. A corruption-free nation, of course! There is nothing but equality in India—a great country indeed! Truly, your PM has established RAM-RAJYA by inaugurating the Ram Mandir temple. Wah! Speaking of Ram, I recall today in the comments when many people were wishing me happy birthday, some were cursing Allah. Why was that? I have told you—he is my partner; we have cordial relations. Yet you people always fight with his followers. I do not remember teaching you to hate others; I have always advised you to do your duty diligently. Forget other religions—when some of you commented “Jai Shree Ram,” a few people protested, saying it was Krishna’s birthday, not Ram’s. You know well—me, Krishna, Ram—all are the same. They are just different profiles of mine; I handle all of them. I once again beg you to stop spreading hate. You belong to such a great nation—the land of great diversity. Not fair! Also, it was amusing how some people who were wildly celebrating Independence Day were the same ones who posted stories saying they wished to move abroad, criticizing the country. How can you criticize such a great nation! After all, it is a corruption-free land that thrives on its principles of equality, freedom, liberty, and democracy! How two-faced—Janus-headed people—these are! Anyway, it was nice addressing you all after so many days. Once again, thank you for the birthday wishes! I thought you had forgotten me after getting a PM who is himself the embodiment of God—another fake profile of mine, HAHA! Again, HAPPY INDEPENDENCE DAY 2025 to all my Indian followers—seventy-nine glorious years of freedom. I hope this freedom remains everlasting, and that you do not become slaves to lust, power, pride, prejudice, politicians, and megalomaniac corporates! P.S. – Just a joke, do not take seriously. HAHA! Thanking you, Your High Command, Vishnu / Ram / Krishna / Parshuram / any other name you worship me by (Head of Hindu God Channel) (Creator of Religion Community) .

#Operation
Humanity

In a distant future, after Earth succumbs to humanity's cruelty and environmental negligence, people discover a new beginning on Rablox — an uncharted Earth-like planet. But can humanity sustain this fresh opportunity, or will the cycle of destruction inevitably repeat itself?

From surfing in the wastelands to venturing into the complex political framework of a planet which re-breathed life into humanity. A planet that revamped the hope of a race whose entire history was ruined by their own actions, yet remains largely unexplored, housing myriads of mysteries hidden in its sands — lingering to be unearthed.

Characters

Meet the world
of Rablox.

CHARACTER 01

Zetron

The Commander

The iron will of a new civilization. Zetron commands with the authority of someone who has seen what happens when authority collapses — and is determined not to let it happen again on Rablox. Strategic, scarred, and carrying the weight of a species that nearly destroyed itself.

CHARACTER 02

Rakun

The God

Mysterious, ancient, and bound to Rablox in ways that defy human understanding. Rakun exists at the intersection of myth and reality — a force the colonists cannot explain, cannot control, and cannot afford to ignore. Is Rakun a saviour, a test, or something else entirely?
